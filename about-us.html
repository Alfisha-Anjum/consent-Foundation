About Us

Welcome to the Consent Foundation, a collaborative initiative committed to advancing data privacy in the digital world. We unite a diverse group of stakeholders including consent managers, data fiduciaries, data processors, data privacy professionals, and technology companies to promote privacy and consent management standards globally.

Our Mission

Privacy is Our Priority

We believe that by empowering individuals and organizations with the right tools and knowledge, we can ensure a safer and more transparent digital world. Our mission is to create positive change by establishing robust privacy standards and fostering a culture of compliance and awareness.

Privacy and Consent Assistance

We provide support and assistance to organizations and individuals to help them navigate privacy and consent challenges in the digital world.

Building Open Source Tools

With passion and purpose, we thrive on developing and providing open-source tools that help manage privacy and consent effectively

Promoting Standards and Compliance

Our team works tirelessly to establish and promote robust standards for privacy and consent management, ensuring compliance with global regulations.

Fostering Data Privacy Awareness

We genuinely care about the well-being of individuals, communities, and organizations by promoting awareness and dialogue on data privacy and compliance.
